Club Penguin

By Fran DiMiceli, Dariush Nashat, Shira Zeev, & Aaron Kates

Fire melts Ice, Ice freezes Water, and Water puts out Fire

Play against a psudo-ai in this strategic card game. Your goal is to get three of one element or one of each element to win. Be careful! You opponent will be trying to do the same, you may want to use a turn to block them from winning instead of furthing your own progress. To select a card, simply click on the card you want and it will place the card down for you.
