Go Apple picking to have enough apple pies for thanksgiving

Do you want to go apple picking to have fresh ingredients for your pies? Click on two colors that are bright and clearly identifiable from anything else in the camera's frame. Once you see the basket and apple picker over your selected colors, bring the apple picker near the top of the screen as if shaking an apple tree. as the apple's decend, try to collect them with the basket. You need to collect 22 apples to make 4 apple pies, if you have collected apples before, try to beat your time.
